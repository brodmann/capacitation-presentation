Capacitation is the penultimate step in the maturation of spermatozoa.


The process of biochemical changes in which the spermatozoa obtain the motility and real fertilizing ability.

Membrane Modification
  • removal of sterols (e.g. cholesterol) from the plasma & acrosomal membrane.
  • leads to more permeability to Ca++ which maybe leads to destabilization of the acrosomal membrane.
Hyperactivation
  • removal of epididymal glycoproteins which inhibits motility of spermatozoa.
  • influx of Ca++ emits associated cell signaling pathways.
